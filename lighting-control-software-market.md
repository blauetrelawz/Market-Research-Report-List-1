<p><ul><li>Visual Control Software</li><li>Photometric Study Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting control software market comprises various types, including visual control software and photometric study software. Visual control software facilitates real-time management of lighting systems, enabling users to control brightness, color, and scheduling for optimal ambiance and energy efficiency. Conversely, photometric study software is used for analyzing and simulating lighting designs, helping professionals assess the impact of light distribution and intensity in a space. Together, they enhance the design, functionality, and efficiency of lighting systems in various applications.</p></p>
