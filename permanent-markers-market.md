<p><p>The permanent markers market is categorized into three main types: chisel tip, bullet tip, and jumbo chisel tip. Chisel tip markers feature a slanted edge that allows for versatile line thickness, making them ideal for both detailed work and bold strokes. Bullet tip markers have a rounded end, providing precise control for fine lines, suitable for writing and drawing. Jumbo chisel tip markers are larger and designed for broader coverage, perfect for marking large surfaces or emphasizing important information. Each type serves distinct purposes based on user needs.</p></p>
