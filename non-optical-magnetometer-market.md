<p><strong>The Non-Optical Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Optical Magnetometer market is divided into two main types: Absolute Magnetometers and Relative Magnetometers. Absolute Magnetometers measure the strength of the magnetic field directly, providing precise values without reference to another magnetometer. In contrast, Relative Magnetometers detect changes in magnetic field strength by comparing readings to a baseline, making them useful for applications like geological surveys and resource exploration. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including aerospace, defense, and geophysics.</p></p>
