<p><ul><li>Horizontal Roller Mill</li><li>Vertical Roller Mill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-roller mills market is primarily segmented into horizontal and vertical roller mills. Horizontal roller mills utilize a pair of horizontally aligned rollers to crush and grind materials, offering efficiency in various industries, including agriculture and food processing. In contrast, vertical roller mills leverage vertically positioned rollers, which are advantageous for grinding materials with a high moisture content and ensure minimal energy consumption. Both types cater to diverse applications, emphasizing the importance of design and equipment choice in optimizing processing capabilities.</p></p>
