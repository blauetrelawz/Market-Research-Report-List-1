<p><strong>The Glass Prepreg Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Prepreg market is divided into thermosetting and thermoplastic types. Thermosetting prepregs are made from resin that hardens upon curing, providing high strength and heat resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Thermoplastic prepregs, on the other hand, remain flexible until heated, allowing for easier processing and recycling. This flexibility enables their use in various industries, including consumer goods and electronics. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the application requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
