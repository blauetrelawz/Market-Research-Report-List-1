<p><p>The Polymeric MDI (Methylene Diphenyl Diisocyanate) market can be categorized into two main types: brown liquid and light brown liquid. Brown liquid MDI typically has a higher viscosity and is often used in rigid foam applications and coatings, providing enhanced durability. Light brown liquid MDI, on the other hand, is more versatile, commonly utilized in flexible foams, adhesives, and sealants. Both types serve crucial functions in various industries, including construction, automotive, and furniture manufacturing, driven by their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
