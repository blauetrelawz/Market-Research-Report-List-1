<p><p>The MRI systems market is primarily divided into open and closed MRI systems. Closed MRI systems feature a cylindrical design that provides stronger magnetic fields, resulting in higher resolution images and faster scans, making them the standard in clinical settings. In contrast, open MRI systems have a more spacious design, reducing patient anxiety and accommodating larger individuals, but typically offer lower magnetic strength and resolution. Both types cater to diverse patient needs and diagnostic requirements within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
