<p><p>Transparent OLED displays are divided into two main types: AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) and PMOLED (Passive Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode). AMOLED displays utilize a matrix of active components for improved efficiency and faster refresh rates, making them ideal for applications requiring high-resolution graphics and interactivity. Conversely, PMOLED displays use a simpler passive matrix, making them less complex and more cost-effective but limited in size and resolution. Both types find applications in various industries like automotive, retail, and advertising.</p></p>
