<p><p>The Artificial Intelligence (AI) Interactive Electronic Whiteboards market is characterized by two main types: front projection and rear projection. Front projection whiteboards are designed to display images from a projector located in front of the board, offering bright and clear visuals suitable for collaboration. In contrast, rear projection whiteboards use projectors positioned behind the screen, minimizing shadows and glare, allowing for a cleaner interactive experience. Both types leverage AI to enhance user engagement and make presentations more dynamic and effective.</p></p>
