<p><p>The oil pipeline leak detection system market comprises several key types, including pressure sensors, flow meters, fiber optic sensors, and acoustic sensors. Pressure sensors monitor pressure changes within the pipeline, indicating potential leaks. Flow meters measure the flow rate of oil to detect inconsistencies. Fiber optic sensors use light transmission to identify disturbances along the pipeline, while acoustic sensors listen for specific sound patterns associated with leaks. Each technology contributes to enhancing safety and minimizing environmental impacts in oil transportation.</p></p>
