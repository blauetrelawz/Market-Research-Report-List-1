<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Adhesive Dispensing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Glue Dispensing Machine</li><li>Hot Melt Dispensing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive dispensing machines are categorized primarily into cold glue and hot melt dispensing machines. Cold glue dispensing machines utilize water-based adhesives that require air-drying, making them suitable for materials sensitive to heat. In contrast, hot melt dispensing machines utilize thermoplastic adhesives that are heated before application, providing fast curing and strong bonding on various surfaces. The choice between these machines depends on application requirements, production speed, and the type of substrates involved in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
