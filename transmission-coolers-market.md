<p><ul><li>Fan Cooling Type</li><li>Water Cooling Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission coolers market can be categorized into two main types: fan cooling type and water cooling type. Fan cooling type systems utilize electric or mechanical fans to increase airflow and enhance heat dissipation from the transmission fluid. In contrast, water cooling type systems use coolant from the engine's cooling system to lower transmission temperatures, providing efficient heat exchange. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal transmission performance and extending vehicle longevity by preventing overheating.</p></p>
