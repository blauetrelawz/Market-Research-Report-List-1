<p><p>The high precision current source market can be categorized into two main types: unipolar and bipolar. Unipolar current sources deliver current in a single direction, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent, stable output, such as in sensor powering. In contrast, bipolar current sources can supply current in both directions, allowing for more versatile applications, such as in waveform generation and signal processing. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing their demand across various industrial and research sectors.</p></p>
