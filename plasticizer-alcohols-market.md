<p><p>The plasticizer alcohols market includes several key types, primarily 2-Ethylhexanol, N-butanol, Isobutanol, and Isononyl alcohol. 2-Ethylhexanol is widely used in the production of phthalate esters, enhancing flexibility and durability in plastics. N-butanol serves as a solvent and in manufacturing butyl esters, while Isobutanol finds application in coatings and adhesives due to its high solvency power. Isononyl alcohol is utilized for producing plasticizers with low volatility and excellent performance in flexible PVC applications. Each type plays a crucial role in different industrial processes.</p></p>
