<p><ul><li>Firmware Over-The-Air Technology (FOTA)</li><li>Software Over-The-Air Technology (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Over the Air (OTA) updates encompass two primary market types: Firmware Over-The-Air Technology (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air Technology (SOTA). FOTA focuses on updating device firmware, allowing manufacturers to remotely enhance system performance, fix bugs, or improve security without user intervention. Conversely, SOTA pertains to software applications, enabling users to access the latest features and improvements seamlessly. Both technologies enhance user experience, reduce service costs, and streamline the update process in diverse sectors such as automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
