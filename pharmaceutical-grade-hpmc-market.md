<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Grade HPMC (Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose) market is categorized into three main types based on viscosity: low, middle, and high viscosity HPMC. Low viscosity HPMC is often used as a binder and thickening agent in formulations requiring easy dispersion. Middle viscosity HPMC serves a balance for controlled-release applications. High viscosity HPMC is primarily utilized in gel formulations and drug delivery systems due to its superior thickening properties. Each type caters to specific pharmaceutical needs, enhancing formulation efficacy and stability.</p></p>
