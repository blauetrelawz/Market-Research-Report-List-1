<p><p>The Fall Arrest Safety Net market is primarily categorized into two types: knotted safety nets and knotless safety nets. Knotted safety nets feature intertwined knots at intersections, providing robust strength and reliability for fall protection. In contrast, knotless safety nets are constructed using a continuous webbing method, resulting in a smoother surface that reduces snagging risks and enhances durability. Both types are essential in construction and industrial settings, ensuring worker safety during height-related tasks.</p></p>
