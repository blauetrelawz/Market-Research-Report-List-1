<p><p>The Polyisoprene Surgical Glove market includes two primary types: powdered sterilized surgical gloves and powder-free sterilized surgical gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with cornstarch, facilitating easier donning, but may pose risks of allergic reactions and contamination. In contrast, powder-free gloves eliminate these concerns and provide improved tactile sensitivity, making them preferred in many medical settings. Both types ensure protection during surgical procedures, but the choice often depends on specific requirements for safety, comfort, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
