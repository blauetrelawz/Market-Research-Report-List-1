<p><p>Educational upright microscopes are categorized into three types based on the number of eyepieces. Monocular microscopes feature a single eyepiece, making them compact and suitable for basic educational purposes. Binocular microscopes offer two eyepieces, providing enhanced comfort and depth perception, ideal for extended use in classrooms. Trinocular microscopes include an additional third eyepiece, allowing for camera attachment, which is beneficial for demonstrations and online learning. Each type serves distinct educational needs, enhancing students' understanding of microscopy.</p></p>
