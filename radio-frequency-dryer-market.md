<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Radio Frequency Dryer</li><li>Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Dryer market is segmented into two main types: Continuous Radio Frequency Dryers and Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryers. Continuous Radio Frequency Dryers operate on a steady flow of material, providing uniform drying over extended periods, ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryers process batches of material, allowing for flexibility in drying times and often catering to smaller operations or specific product requirements. Both types leverage radio frequency technology to enhance drying efficiency and reduce processing time.</p></p>
