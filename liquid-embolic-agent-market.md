The 6% EVOH agents are typically used for more precise embolization, targeting smaller vessels with reduced risk of collateral damage. In contrast, 8% EVOH agents offer a denser formulation, allowing for quicker occlusion of larger vessels. These types cater to varying clinical needs in interventional radiology and neurosurgery, enhancing treatment efficacy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
