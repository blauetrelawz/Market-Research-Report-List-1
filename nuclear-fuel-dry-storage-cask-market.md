<p><p>Nuclear fuel dry storage casks are essential for the safe, long-term containment of spent nuclear fuel. There are two primary market types: horizontal dry casks and vertical dry casks. Horizontal dry casks are typically larger and designed to hold multiple fuel assemblies laid down in a horizontal position, optimizing space and facilitating transport. In contrast, vertical dry casks store fuel vertically, allowing for a more compact footprint and easier access for maintenance. Both designs prioritize safety and efficiency in storage solutions.</p></p>
