<p><p>The ingot lifter market comprises three primary types: magnetic ingot holders, horizontal ingot clips, and vertical ingot clips. Magnetic ingot holders utilize magnets to securely grip metal ingots during handling, ensuring stability and safety. Horizontal ingot clips are designed to hold ingots horizontally, facilitating efficient transport and storage. Vertical ingot clips, on the other hand, allow for vertical lifting, optimizing space and accessibility in manufacturing facilities. Each type serves specific operational needs within metalworking and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
