<p><p>Plasma disinfection and sterilization purification equipment encompasses various technologies aimed at eliminating microorganisms. Plasma sterilization utilizes ionized gases to disrupt cellular structures, making it effective for heat-sensitive materials. Electrostatic sterilization employs charged particles to attract and neutralize contaminants. UV sterilization uses ultraviolet light to damage microbial DNA, rendering them inactive. Other methods may include thermochemical or vapor-phase techniques. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in healthcare, laboratories, and food processing, ensuring enhanced safety and hygiene standards.</p></p>
