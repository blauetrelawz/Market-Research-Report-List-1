<p><p>The residential roof skylights market comprises three main types: manual skylights, power skylights, and securing skylights. Manual skylights are opened and closed manually, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Power skylights utilize electric or solar-powered mechanisms for convenient operation and can include sensors for automatic adjustment. Securing skylights are designed with enhanced safety features, focusing on durability and theft prevention, often incorporating impact-resistant materials. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences based on functionality and security.</p></p>
