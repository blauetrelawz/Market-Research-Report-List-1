<p><p>The thermal power steam turbine market encompasses various types, primarily including condensing and back pressure turbines. Condensing turbines utilize steam at low pressure, allowing for efficient energy conversion and maximizing electricity generation. In contrast, back pressure turbines operate at higher pressure, supplying steam for process heating in industrial applications while generating power. Other types may include extraction turbines, which draw steam at intermediate stages. Together, these variations cater to diverse energy needs across industries, optimizing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
