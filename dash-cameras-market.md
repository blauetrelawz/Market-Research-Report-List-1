<p><p>The dash camera market is divided into two primary types: single-channel and multi-channel dash cameras. Single-channel dash cameras capture video footage from one perspective, typically the front of the vehicle, providing essential evidence in case of accidents. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras offer coverage from multiple angles, such as front and rear, or even interior views, enhancing security and surveillance. This diversification allows users to choose based on their specific needs for safety, evidence collection, and monitoring while driving.</p></p>
