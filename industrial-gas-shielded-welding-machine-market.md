<p><ul><li>Inert Gas</li><li>Non-Inert Gas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Gas Shielded Welding Machine market is categorized into two main types: inert gas and non-inert gas systems. Inert gas welding, commonly using argon or helium, provides a stable and contaminant-free shield, ideal for metals like aluminum and titanium, ensuring high-quality welds. Non-inert gas welding, which utilizes active gases like carbon dioxide, is often employed in more common applications such as mild steel welding. Each type serves distinct industries and materials, tailored to specific welding requirements.</p></p>
