<p><strong>The Aluminum Paste for Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leafing Aluminium Paste</li><li>Non-Leafing Aluminium Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum paste for printing ink market is divided into two main types: leafing aluminum paste and non-leafing aluminum paste. Leafing aluminum paste allows for a metallic, reflective finish by floating particles on the surface of the ink, enhancing visual brilliance. Conversely, non-leafing aluminum paste disperses evenly throughout the ink, providing a more muted and consistent color. Both types are used in various printing applications, including packaging and decorative printing, catering to different aesthetic and performance requirements.</p></p>
