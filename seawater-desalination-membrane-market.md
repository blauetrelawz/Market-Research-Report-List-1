<p><p>The seawater desalination membrane market comprises several key types, including reverse osmosis membranes, electrodialysis membranes, and other specialized membranes. Reverse osmosis membranes are widely used due to their efficiency in separating salt and impurities from seawater through pressure-driven processes. Electrodialysis membranes utilize an electric field to move ions selectively, which is effective for lower salinity sources. Other membrane types may include nanofiltration and forward osmosis membranes, each offering unique advantages tailored to specific desalination needs and technology advancements.</p></p>
