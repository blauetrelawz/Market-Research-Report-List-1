<p><p>The Automatic Wrap Around Case Packer market is divided into two types: continuous and intermittent. Continuous case packers operate non-stop, efficiently handling high-volume production with minimal downtime, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Intermittent case packers, on the other hand, work in batches, pausing between cycles to facilitate adjustments or changeovers. This type is suitable for businesses with varying product sizes and lower volume needs, allowing for more flexibility and customization during packing processes.</p></p>
