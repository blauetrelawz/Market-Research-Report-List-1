<p><ul><li>Single Rotor Type</li><li>Coaxial Rotor Type</li><li>Tandem Rotor Type</li><li>Quad-Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Helicopter UAV market encompasses various types based on rotor configurations. Single Rotor Type features one main rotor, providing efficiency and stability but requiring complex tail rotors. Coaxial Rotor Type employs two rotors stacked vertically, enhancing lift and maneuverability while minimizing noise. Tandem Rotor Type utilizes two large rotors positioned fore and aft, ideal for heavy lifting and stability. Quad-Rotor Type, commonly known as quadcopters, features four rotors, offering simplicity, ease of control, and flexibility for diverse applications.</p></p>
