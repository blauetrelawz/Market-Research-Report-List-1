<p><p>The metal furnace market is categorized into batch furnaces and continuous furnaces. Batch furnaces are designed to process a specific amount of material at a time, allowing for flexibility in production and suited for varied product types. In contrast, continuous furnaces operate in a constant flow, efficiently handling large volumes of material with minimal interruptions. This distinction influences energy consumption, operational costs, and production scalability, catering to different industrial needs within the metal processing sector.</p></p>
