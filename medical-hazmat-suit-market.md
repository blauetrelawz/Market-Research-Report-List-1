<p><p>The medical hazmat suit market is categorized into two primary types: light hazmat suits and heavy hazmat suits. Light hazmat suits offer basic protection against low-level exposure to hazardous materials, suitable for non-toxic environments or short-term use. They prioritize mobility and comfort, making them ideal for healthcare workers in routine situations. In contrast, heavy hazmat suits provide maximum protection against highly hazardous substances, designed for emergency response or containment scenarios, featuring reinforced materials and advanced filtration systems for comprehensive safety.</p></p>
