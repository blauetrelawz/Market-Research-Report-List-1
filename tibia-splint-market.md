<p><strong>The Tibia Splint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stabilization</li><li>Traction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tibia splint market comprises two primary types: stabilization and traction. Stabilization splints are designed to immobilize the lower leg, ensuring proper alignment and minimizing movement during the healing process. They are crucial for acute injuries and post-surgical recovery. In contrast, traction splints provide steady pull on the bone, aiding in alignment during fractures. This method is beneficial in emergency situations to reduce pain and prevent further injury. Both types play essential roles in effective tibial fracture management.</p></p>
