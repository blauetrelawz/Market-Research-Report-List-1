<p><strong>The Metallurgical Blower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blower</li><li>Positive Displacement Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallurgical blower market consists primarily of two types: centrifugal blowers and positive displacement blowers. Centrifugal blowers utilize rotating impellers to generate airflow, making them suitable for high-volume applications in metallurgical processes, such as cooling or combustion air supply. In contrast, positive displacement blowers trap a specific volume of air and force it into the desired application, offering high pressure and consistent flow, ideal for pneumatic conveying of materials. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing efficiency and performance in metallurgical operations.</p></p>
