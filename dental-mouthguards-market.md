<p><p>The dental mouthguard market comprises three main types: preformed, thermoformed, and custom mouthguards. Preformed mouthguards are ready-to-use and typically sold in standard sizes, offering basic protection. Thermoformed mouthguards are soft and moldable, designed by heating the material for a personalized fit, providing better comfort and protection. Custom mouthguards are individually crafted by dental professionals, ensuring precise fit and optimal performance for athletes or those requiring specific dental protection. Each type addresses different needs for safety and comfort.</p></p>
