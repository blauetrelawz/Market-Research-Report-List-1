<p><strong>The Rat Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Isolator</li><li>Negative Pressure Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rat isolator market comprises Positive Pressure Isolators and Negative Pressure Isolators, each serving distinct purposes in laboratory and research settings. Positive Pressure Isolators protect the contents from external contaminants by maintaining higher internal pressure, ensuring a sterile environment. In contrast, Negative Pressure Isolators draw air inward, preventing contamination from the interior to the outside, primarily used for handling hazardous substances. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and compliance in biomedical research and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
