<p><p>The Protein Production Service Market encompasses various expression systems used for producing proteins. Mammalian cell expression systems are ideal for complex proteins requiring post-translational modifications, crucial for therapeutic applications. Yeast cell expression systems combine the benefits of prokaryotic and eukaryotic systems, offering robust yields and glycosylation capabilities.
