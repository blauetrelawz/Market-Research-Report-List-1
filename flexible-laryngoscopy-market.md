<p><p>The flexible laryngoscopy market is divided into two main types: single-use and reusable flexible laryngoscopes. Single-use flexible laryngoscopes are designed for one-time use, enhancing infection control and eliminating the need for sterilization, making them convenient in urgent care and outpatient settings.
